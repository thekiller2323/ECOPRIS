Project description

It will be an online prision staff self-assessment on skills addressed by the project: mannagement, entrepreneurship, prison work and industries awareness, ecology, marketing, communication, ICT, teamwork and labour market integrated reintegration, this assessment tool will be deliver a skill report allowing understanding target group skills gasps. The output includes a skills assessment survey witch will be included in the learning management system and witch will be applied to training applicants. This output can be used by other prisons and prison systems.

Defenition of Skills:

Management

Using appropriate procedures to collect, organize, retrive, maintain and diseminate information, prediction, control, monitoring, and evaluation, risk management. Manages
